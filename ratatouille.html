Ratatouille


Ingredients

  • 1c Marinara
  • 1/2 Yellow onion
  • 1/2 Eggplant
  • 1 Yellow squash
  • 1 Zucchini
  • 3 Roma tomatoes
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Olive oil
  • Salt & pepper
  • Mozzrerella

Directions

Step 1

Preheat Oven 400*F

Step 2

Slice veggies

Step 3

Place marinara on bottom of pan

Step 4

Sprinkle onions on top
