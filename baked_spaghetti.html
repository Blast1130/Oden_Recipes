Watson Family Baked Spaghetti

Yields 10 servings

Ingredients

  • 1lb Hamburger
  • 1can spaghetti sauce
  • 1pkg spaghetti noodles DO NOT BREAK
  • 2c cheddar cheese
  • 1can cream of celery soup
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 small onion
  • parmesan cheese
  • black olives optional

Directions

Step 1

Preheat Oven 375`F

Step 2

Brown Hamburger and drain

Step 3

Dice onion and green pepper

Step 4

Add diced to burger

Step 5

Cook down diced items

Step 6

Add olives and sauce to mix

Step 7

Cook noodles Do not drain

Steps 8
In 13x9" pan

Layer
1/2 noodles then 1/2 sauce

Add ALL THE CHEESE

Layer
The rest of the noodles then the rest of the sauce

Step 9
In bowl

Add cream of celery soup and 1 can of water so its creamy

Step 10

Pour bowl mix evenly onto the pan mix

Step 11

Top with parmesan cheese

Step 12

Cover with tinfoil

Step 13

Bake for 20 minutes

Step 14

Enjoy
